Welcome to Privorum - a privacy first blockchain.

Privorum is a fork of Ethereum, upgraded to be Open, Neutral, Borderless, Censorship-resistant, Autonomous, Decentralized alternative for enterprises.
It’s biggest asset is the private smart contracts which are designed to reverse engineer data management as we know it.
It allows individuals to keep full control over their personal data in their interactions with both public and private organisations.

As such this solution does not only solve the issue of data breaches and hacks, identity theft and the incredibly high cost of cybersecurity and data protection activities, but it will also allow organisations to profile themselves as a trustworthy party to their increasingly suspicious and critical clients, users and citizens. Saving time and money, gaining trust.

We’ve built Privorum with everything you need.

Strong privacy

Private information is never broadcast to network participants. Private data is encrypted and only shared directly with relevant parties.

Use-case specific consensus

Pick a consensus algorithm to suit your requirements: MPOS, POSDAO.

Permissioning

Flexibly define how to onboard entities and users onto your network using Privorum’s smart-contract based permissions model.

Simple integration

With privorum-cloud, you can easily deploy Privorum across different cloud environments. Or, use Docker for seamless cross-environment integration.

Compatibility

We work with the existing tools you know: Truffle, MetaMask, Remix and OpenZeppelin (to name a few) – they’re ready to use

Open source

Priorum is 100% open source and its based on Polkadot, so you can develop with confidence.

Why are we introducing Privorum?

Data leaks, data leaks everywhere. We are worried about the growing number of data breaches, identity theft and abuse of personal data. Any centralised database can be hacked, and your data can be exposed.

Act now

Let’s change this obsolete system!

Why not change this system on its head, making you the sole gatekeeper of who you want to share your data with and under which terms

Identity theft & fraud It avoids fraud, identity theft, data overload from the perspective of the individual but also it mitigates risk of breach or potential GDPR fines and as such cost, time, resources and most importantly trust issues of organisations.

Why we built Privorum ? To change the way personal data is being currently stored and shared by allowing individuals to retain full control over it, moving the storage from corporations to individuals and as such safeguarding personal data from data breaches, identity theft, unnecessary duplication and abuse.

A fully secure and private digital identity wallet to manage your Personal data You can update, add and even change your personal data at any given time. You can give organisations temporary access to the relevant data under specific terms (for instance you can give access to your data for verification and then this data is being deleted again by you removing the access)
